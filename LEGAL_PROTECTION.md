# AI4U Legal Protection Layer

## Overview
The Legal Protection Layer establishes the formal terms of use, licensing agreements, and metadata embedding required to protect AI4U, LLC, its users, and the intellectual property generated by the Little Engineer platform. It ensures compliance, limits liability, and clearly defines the rights associated with AI-generated 3D designs.

## Components

### 1. Terms of Use (`/terms`)
A comprehensive legal document outlining the rules for using the AI4U Little Engineer platform.

**Key Provisions:**
- **Acceptance of Terms:** Establishes a legally binding agreement between the user and AI4U, LLC (Lee Hanna, Owner).
- **AI-Generated Content Disclaimer:** Explicitly states that designs are AI-generated and AI4U makes no warranty regarding their fitness, structural soundness, or safety. Users assume all risk.
- **Intellectual Property:** Clarifies ownership of user inputs, grants licenses for generated outputs, and protects AI4U's proprietary systems (VPL, Trust Policy Engine, KeyGuardian).
- **User Conduct:** Prohibits the generation of weapons, illegal devices, or attempts to bypass platform security.
- **Marketplace Rules:** Defines the requirements for selling designs (must achieve "Verified Commercial" trust tier) and the finality of sales.
- **Limitation of Liability:** Protects AI4U from indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of the service or printed designs.

### 2. Marketplace License Terms (`/marketplace/license`)
A specific agreement governing the purchase and use of designs from the AI4U marketplace.

**Key Provisions:**
- **License Grant:** Buyers receive a perpetual, non-exclusive, worldwide license to use the design files for personal and commercial manufacturing.
- **Permitted Uses:** Printing for personal use, commercial sale of physical objects, and modification for personal use.
- **Prohibited Uses:** Reselling or redistributing the original digital files, claiming authorship, removing AI4U metadata, or using the design for harmful purposes.
- **Quality Assurance Disclaimer:** Explains the VPL validation score but reiterates that print success depends on the user's specific equipment and environment.
- **Refund Policy:** Defines the limited circumstances under which refunds are issued (e.g., corrupted files, material misrepresentation).

### 3. Project Metadata Embedding
To ensure traceability and protect the platform's reputation, critical metadata is embedded into every generated design record in the database.

**Metadata Fields (added via migration `009_brand_visual_layer.sql`):**
- `origin`: Always set to `ai_generated`.
- `validated`: Boolean indicating if the design has passed VPL validation.
- `validated_at`: Timestamp of validation.
- `vpl_grade`: The assigned grade (A-F).
- `trust_tier`: The assigned trust tier (e.g., `trusted_commercial`).

This metadata is displayed on the Marketplace License page and is intended to be included in any exported design files (e.g., as comments in the G-code or metadata in the STL/STEP files, though this specific file embedding is a future enhancement).

### 4. AppFooter Component
A minimal, globally accessible footer (`AppFooter.tsx`) that provides links to the legal pages and displays the required copyright notice: `© [Year] AI4U, LLC. AI4Utech.com, Lee Hanna-Owner. All rights reserved.`

## Design Philosophy
The legal layer is designed to be transparent, accessible, and robust. The language is formal but clear, ensuring users understand their rights and responsibilities while maximizing protection for AI4U, LLC. The integration of metadata directly into the design records provides a technical safeguard against misrepresentation.
