# Click-to-Make Gallery

The **Click-to-Make Gallery** (`/gallery`) is a curated grid of 16 featured project cards on the AI4U Little Engineer platform.

## Purpose

The gallery serves two primary functions:
1. **Inspiration**: Showcasing the breadth of what the platform can generate, from precision mechanical parts to fun desk toys.
2. **Immediate Action**: Providing a one-click path to generate a real, printable design.

## Structure

The gallery is organized into four distinct categories, reflecting the dual-lane architecture of the platform:

1. **Precision Parts (Shop Lane)**: Exact-fit mechanical components (e.g., spacers, brackets, jigs).
2. **Fun Prints (Fun Lane)**: Toys, gadgets, and desk accessories (e.g., catapults, fidget spinners).
3. **Showcase & Demos**: Featured designs and platform demonstrations (e.g., Artemis II display base).
4. **Gift & Decor**: Personalized items and home accessories (e.g., custom signs, planters).

## The "Make This" Flow

Each card in the gallery represents a real, generatable design, not just a concept image.

When a user clicks the "Make This" button on a card, they are directed to the Universal Input Composer (`/invent`) with the card's specific prompt pre-filled.

This seamless transition from browsing to creating lowers the barrier to entry and encourages immediate engagement with the core generation engine.
